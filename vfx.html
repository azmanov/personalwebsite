VFX // Filmmaking

I've been makin' movies for as long as I can remember. It all started with iMovie. Ahhh the good old days. Even the tiniest edits were a pain in the ass and nothing ever looked good. My film making career then escalated massively when I accidentally bought a 300$ version of Final Cut X on my dad's credit card (He wasn't delighted, to say the least).

I learned that paying money for software is the wrong way to go, so I am currently using pirated versions of Premiere Pro and After Effects (both CS6) to do my editing and visual effects. I also use Apple's Logic Pro X for my music. I have always been focused on very short VFX-driven films, but I am hoping to someday make longer films, that tell a full story from start to finish.

Recently, I participated in the Green-GO short film contest . I didn't win, by I definitely enjoyed the experience. Click the link to check out my submission and tell me what you think!
